Rick and Morty Themed Hangman Game

Welcome to the Rick and Morty hangman game. Try and guess which character's name is being displayed, by guessing letters from the alphabet. If a guess is correct, the correct letter will replace the underscore currently in it's place. If the guess is incorrect, it will display below the word in a word bank of guessed letters. You will also loose a life. You have 6 lives to guess the word correctly.
